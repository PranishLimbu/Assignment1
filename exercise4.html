about efootball

You have a ton of options on how to play, such as the "Authentic Team" that lets you use real clubs and national teams. There is also the "Dream Team" where you can choose your favourite footballers, whether they are active players or football legends, to create your original team. This is the game where real-life football and your wildest football dreams unite!
